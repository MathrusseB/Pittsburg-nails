## How to use this menu in the build

**Services section on the page:** Show pedicures, manicures, and enhancements expanded by default (these are the high-intent services). Put dipping powder, princess menu, nail art, additional services, waxing, and eyelash extensions inside `<details>` accordions so the page doesn't become a wall of pricing.

**Booking form service dropdown:** Use a grouped `<select>` with `<optgroup>` labels matching the categories above. Group order: Pedicures, Manicures, Nail Enhancements, Dipping Powder, Princess Menu, Nail Art, Waxing, Eyelash Extensions, Additional Services. End with `<option>Not sure yet — call to discuss</option>`.

**For the schema markup:** Don't list every service in JSON-LD (overkill). Include `priceRange: "$$"` on the LocalBusiness object and that's enough.

**Display rule:** Always show Cash and Card prices side by side. The card price is the cash price + 2% (the salon passes the processing fee — it's intentional, don't hide it).
