Welcome

A small salon that takes its time.

Pittsburg Nail Spa has been part of the neighborhood since opening on North Broadway. We do clean, careful work — classic manicures, real pedicures with hot towels, gel and acrylic when you want them, and nail art for when you’re feeling it.

Walk in, sit down, leave with hands and feet you’re proud of. Complimentary water, soft drinks, and juice while you’re with us.

Questions

Frequently asked.

How do I book an appointment?

Fill out the form above and we’ll get a text with your request, or just call (620) 715-0115. Walk-ins are welcome too — we’ll fit you in if a chair is open.

Do you provide everything for the treatment?

Yes. We supply all tools, polishes, and products. Just show up — we take it from there.

Do you offer services for children?

Yes. Our Princess Menu is designed for guests 10 and under, with shorter services and kid-friendly pricing. See the full menu above.

Can I request a specific technician?

Absolutely. Mention their name when you book and we’ll do our best to match your preferred schedule.
